Wright Brothers

Orville Wright and Wilbur Wright

They are american aviation pioneers generally credited with inventing, building, and flying the world's first successful airplane.

Wilbur and Orville Wright taught the world to fly. With courage and perseverance, these self-taught engineers relied on teamwork and application of the scientific process. What they achieved changed our world forever.

Both brothers did not receive their highschool diploma and was not able to study college but it was not a hindrance for them to do what they want and because of hardwork and how determined the brothers are, they are known as inventor and succeeded in flying the world's first airplane with the wright flyer
